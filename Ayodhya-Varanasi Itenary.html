Ayodhya & Varanasi Itinerary

Trip from Bangalore (November 30 - December 3)

Itinerary Overview

Travel Dates: November 30 - December 3

Flights: Air India Express : IX-2508 (BLR T2-AYJ), Air India Express : IX-2716 (VNS-BLR T2)

  • Departure: November 30, 8:55 AM from Bangalore
  • Arrival in Ayodhya: November 30, 12:05 PM
  • Return Flight: December 3, 9:45 AM from Varanasi

Day 1: Arrival in Ayodhya

  • 12:05 PM: Arrive in Ayodhya.
  • 1:00 PM: Check into the hotel.
  • 2:00 PM: Explore Ayodhya: Ram Janmabhoomi Complex, Hanuman Garhi, Nageshwarnath Temple.
  • 8:00 PM: Dinner and rest at the hotel.

Day 2: Travel to Varanasi

  • 8:00 AM: Breakfast and check out.
  • 11:30 AM: Arrival in Varanasi and check into the hotel.
  • Afternoon: Visit Sarnath and Banaras Hindu University.
  • 6:00 PM: Attend Ganga Aarti at Dashashwamedh Ghat.

Day 3: Full Day in Varanasi

  • 7:00 AM: Breakfast at the hotel.
  • Morning: Visit Annapurna Temple and Manikarnika Ghat.
  • Afternoon: Explore Assi Ghat, Namo Ghat and Tulsi Manas Temple.
  • 9:00 PM: VIP Darshan at Kashi Vishwanath Temple, after dinner.

Day 4: Departure from Varanasi

  • 7:00 AM: Breakfast at the hotel.
  • 8:00 AM: Check out and head to Varanasi airport.
  • 9:45 AM: Flight back to Bangalore.
